# Book Notes Index

A lightweight index of books I've taken structured notes on. For deeper atomic notes, see [[../../vault-2-Zettelkasten/30-Structure/MOC-Cognition]].

## 2026

### Thinking, Fast and Slow — Daniel Kahneman
- **Key idea**: System 1 (fast, intuitive) vs System 2 (slow, deliberate). Most errors come from System 1 overriding contexts that require System 2.
- **Standout concept**: Availability heuristic — ease of recall ≠ frequency. Plane crashes feel more common than car accidents because of media salience.
- **Application**: Product decision-making — run pre-mortems to engage System 2 before committing.
- **Podcast Ep 1 notes**: [[Projects/Podcast-Series]]

### Four Thousand Weeks — Oliver Burkeman
- **Key idea**: You have ~4,000 weeks of life. Productivity optimisation is often avoidance of confronting finitude.
- **Standout concept**: "The efficiency trap" — the more efficiently you work, the more work expands to fill capacity.
- **Application**: Stop treating leisure as recovery for more work. Protect it directly.

### The Making of a Manager — Julie Zhuo
- **Key idea**: Management is learned, not innate. The job is to build a team that gets great outcomes.
- **Standout**: Feedback model — specific, behavioural, forward-looking.
- **Application**: Applied to APM mentoring — Luca and Priya. See [[Areas/Career-Development]].

## 2025

### Atomic Habits — James Clear
- **Key idea**: Habits are formed by cue → craving → response → reward loop. Identity precedes behaviour.
- **Standout**: "You don't rise to the level of your goals, you fall to the level of your systems."
- **Application**: Morning routine, exercise consistency. See [[Areas/Health-Fitness]].

### Deep Work — Cal Newport
- **Key idea**: Cognitively demanding work requires long, uninterrupted blocks. Shallow work expands to fill available time.
- **Application**: Time-block 08:00–12:00 as protected deep work. Applied to [[Projects/Q3-Product-Launch]] spec writing.
