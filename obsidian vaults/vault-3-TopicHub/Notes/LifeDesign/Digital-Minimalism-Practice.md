# Digital Minimalism Practice

> Personal implementation of Newport's digital minimalism. Companion to [[Deep-Work-Protocol]].

## Philosophy

Technology should serve my values, not fragment my attention. I keep tools that do something nothing else can do; I remove everything else.

## Current Tool Inventory

### Kept (and why)

| Tool | Kept because | Constraints |
|---|---|---|
| RSS via Miniflux | Pull-only, curated, no algorithm | Read only at 08:00–08:20 |
| Obsidian | Thinking environment, local-first, no social | Unlimited |
| Slack | Required for Apex work | Notifications off; check at 12:00 and 16:30 |
| Whatsapp | Primary family communication | Personal phone only; no desktop app |
| Calendar | Required coordination tool | Notifications off except 10min before meetings |

### Removed

| Tool | Removed | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Twitter/X | March 2026 | Variable-ratio reinforcement; low-value content; see [[../../vault-2-Zettelkasten/20-Permanent/perm-0053]] |
| LinkedIn feed | January 2026 | Feed removed via uBlock; still accessible for direct messages |
| Podcast autoplay | April 2026 | Was eliminating solitude in commutes; see [[../../vault-2-Zettelkasten/20-Permanent/perm-0052]] |
| YouTube recommendations | February 2026 | Replaced with deliberate search only (no home feed via extension) |

## Phone Protocol

- **Weekday mornings**: Phone in bedroom until 12:00
- **Evenings**: Phone on charger by 21:30
- **Notifications**: Only calls and calendar. Everything else: manual check
- **No social apps on work phone**

## Results (3 months in)

- Deep work hours/week: ↑ from ~6h to ~18h (overlaps with [[Deep-Work-Protocol]])
- Anxiety levels: noticeably lower (subjective)
- Reading pace: ↑ from 1.5 to 2 books/month
- Newsletter [[../../vault-1-PARA/Projects/Newsletter-Growth]] open rate: ↑ (better writing quality from more focused work)
