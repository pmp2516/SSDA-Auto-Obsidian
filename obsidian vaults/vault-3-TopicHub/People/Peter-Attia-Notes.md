# Peter Attia — Notes

Physician specialising in longevity medicine. Podcast "The Drive" + book "Outlive" (2023).

**Core framework**: Healthspan over lifespan. Four "horsemen" of chronic disease: cardiovascular disease, cancer, metabolic dysfunction, neurodegeneration. Prevention via exercise, nutrition, sleep, mental health.

**Key ideas applied to [[../Hubs/Life-Design-Hub]]**:
- Zone 2 cardio as the foundation of metabolic health — 3–4 hours/week at conversational pace
- Strength training for muscle mass as a longevity lever (not just aesthetics)
- Sleep as the master variable — everything downstream degrades with <7h

- VO2 max tracking → [[../../vault-1-PARA/Areas/Health-Fitness]]
- Strength programme → [[../../vault-1-PARA/Resources/Training-Log]]
- Supplements: magnesium glycinate, vitamin D, omega-3 (from Attia)
