## Structure

### Cold open (0:00–1:00)
Hook. A surprising fact, a provocative question, or a vivid scenario from the book.

### Intro / housekeeping (1:00–2:30)
"Welcome to Slow Reads. I'm [name]…" Brief episode summary.

### Part 1 — The author and context (2:30–8:00)
Who wrote this, when, why it mattered. Set up the central problem the book addresses.

### Part 2 — Core ideas (8:00–22:00)
3 key ideas, each with:
- The idea stated clearly (1 sentence)
- How the author argues for it
- One concrete example from the book
- My reaction / pushback

### Part 3 — Personal application (22:00–30:00)
How has this book actually changed what I do? Specific examples from work and life.

### Wrap-up (30:00–35:00)
- Who should read this (and who shouldn't)
- Rating out of 5
- What I'm reading next
- Outro music cue
