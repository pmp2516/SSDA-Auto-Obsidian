# AI & ML Reading List

> [!info] Purpose
> Curated reading with brief annotations. Used for: staying sharp technically, feeding [[Projects/Q3-Product-Launch]] positioning, and [[Areas/Career-Development]] evidence of technical depth.

## Papers — 2026

### Foundational
- **"Attention Is All You Need"** (Vaswani et al., 2017) — the original transformer paper. Re-read annually. Core mental model for Meridian's AI suggestion engine.
- **"Scaling Laws for Neural Language Models"** (Kaplan et al., 2020) — compute/data/params tradeoffs. Referenced in Q3 feature scoping.
- **"Constitutional AI: Harmlessness from AI Feedback"** (Anthropic, 2022) — RLHF alternative via self-critique. Relevant to trust/safety positioning for [[Projects/Q3-Product-Launch]].

### Recent (2025–2026)
- **"Flash Attention 2"** (Dao, 2023) — 2× faster attention, crucial for inference cost. Direct relevance to AI suggestion engine latency budget.
- **"Mixture of Experts: A Survey"** (Fedus et al., 2024) — sparse activation; how Mixtral/GPT-4 class models route tokens. 
- **"Long-form Factuality in LLMs"** (Google DeepMind, 2025) — hallucination mitigation. Added to positioning notes for AI features.
- **"Mechanistic Interpretability at Scale"** (Anthropic, 2026) — circuit analysis in large models. Early read; not yet actionable.
