# Fleeting note — 2026-05-23 07:40

Morning thought: the reason I resist writing first drafts is not fear of failure but fear of *closure*. Once it's written, the idea is finite. In my head it's still perfect and infinite.

The written version will always be a reduction of the mental version. And I know this in advance. So there's grief built into writing.

Workaround: treat the first draft as a *question*, not an answer. "Here is what I think I mean" rather than "here is what I mean."
